<p><p>Wireless ECG devices are categorized into two main types: monitoring ECG systems and diagnostic ECG systems. Monitoring ECG systems are primarily used for continuous, real-time tracking of a patient's heart activity, often in home or remote settings, enabling proactive health management. On the other hand, diagnostic ECG systems are designed for detailed analysis and interpretation of cardiac conditions, typically employed in clinical environments to assist healthcare professionals in diagnosing heart diseases through recorded ECG data.</p></p>
