<p><p>The dye-sensitized solar cell (DSSC) market is segmented into two main types based on electrolyte composition: organic solvent electrolyte-based and ionic liquid electrolyte-based cells. Organic solvent electrolyte DSSCs typically utilize volatile solvents, offering higher efficiency but posing stability and environmental concerns. In contrast, ionic liquid electrolyte DSSCs feature non-volatile, thermally stable ionic liquids, which enhance thermal stability and efficiency while improving safety. The choice between these types affects overall performance, manufacturing costs, and potential applications in renewable energy.</p></p>
