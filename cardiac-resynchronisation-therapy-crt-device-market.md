The market comprises two main types: CRT pacemakers, which provide pacing to synchronize heart rhythms, and CRT defibrillators, which combine pacing with the ability to deliver shocks for arrhythmias. Both devices help enhance cardiac function, improve symptoms, and reduce hospitalization, catering to diverse patient needs in the management of heart failure and related conditions.</p></p>
