<p><p>The paper coating materials market encompasses various materials enhancing paper quality. GCC (Ground Calcium Carbonate) offers brightness and opacity, while PCC (Precipitated Calcium Carbonate) provides fine particle distribution for smooth surfaces. Kaolin clay improves printability and smoothness. Talc adds opaqueness and reduces gloss, while starch serves as a binder for strength and flexibility in coatings. SB latex provides adhesion and flexibility, and titanium dioxide significantly enhances brightness and whiteness. Wax imparts water resistance and improves surface finish, making substrates more durable.</p></p>
