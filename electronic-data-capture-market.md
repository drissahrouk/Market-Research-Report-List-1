<p><p>The Electronic Data Capture (EDC) market encompasses three primary types: web-hosted, licensed enterprise, and cloud-based solutions. Web-hosted EDC systems are accessed via the internet, offering flexibility and ease of use. Licensed enterprise systems are installed locally on company servers, providing greater control but requiring significant infrastructure. Cloud-based EDC solutions utilize off-site servers, enabling scalable, cost-effective access and enhanced collaboration. These types cater to different organizational needs, balancing control, accessibility, and scalability in clinical data management.</p></p>
