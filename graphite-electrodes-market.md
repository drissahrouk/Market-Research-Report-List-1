<p><p>The graphite electrodes market comprises three main types: Regular Power, High Power, and Ultra High Power (UHP) electrodes. Regular Power electrodes are used in various classical electric arc furnace (EAF) applications, while High Power electrodes, with enhanced electrical and thermal conductivity, are suited for higher energy requirements. UHP electrodes offer the highest performance with superior conductivity and thermal resistance, tailored for demanding applications like secondary steelmaking. Each type serves distinct needs within metallurgical industries, driving their market dynamics.</p></p>
