<p><p>2D code readers can be categorized into two types based on their field of vision: narrow field of vision and wide field of vision. Narrow field of vision readers are designed for precision scanning of small, specific areas, making them ideal for applications like retail checkout or industrial automation where items are closely situated. Conversely, wide field of vision readers can capture larger areas, allowing them to scan multiple codes or objects simultaneously, suitable for environments like warehouses or logistics.</p></p>
