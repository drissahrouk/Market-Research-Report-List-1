<p><p>The high-temperature composite resin market encompasses several key types, each serving specific applications. Phenolic resins offer excellent thermal stability and fire resistance, making them ideal for aerospace and automotive industries. Epoxy resins are known for their strong adhesive properties and versatility, commonly used in structural applications. Thermoplastics are favored for their ease of processing and recyclability, while polyimides provide exceptional thermal and chemical resistance.
