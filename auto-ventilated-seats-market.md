<p><strong>The Auto Ventilated Seats Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial Fans Seats</li><li>Axial Fans Seats</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The auto ventilated seats market is primarily categorized into two types: radial fan seats and axial fan seats. Radial fan seats utilize fans that move air in a radial direction, providing efficient airflow and cooling across a broader area, enhancing comfort during long drives. In contrast, axial fan seats feature fans that direct air parallel to the fan axis, offering targeted ventilation. Both types improve the driving experience by regulating seat temperature and ensuring optimal comfort in various climates.</p></p>
