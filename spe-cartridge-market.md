<p><strong>The SPE Cartridge Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reversed-Phase</li><li>Ion-Exchange</li><li>Normal Phase</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Solid Phase Extraction (SPE) cartridges are categorized into three primary types: Reversed-Phase, Ion-Exchange, and Normal Phase. Reversed-Phase cartridges utilize hydrophobic interactions for separating non-polar compounds, making them ideal for a wide range of organic molecules. Ion-Exchange cartridges target ionic species via electrostatic interactions, effectively separating charged compounds. Normal Phase cartridges, characterized by a polar stationary phase, are suited for separating polar and non-polar compounds, operating on adsorption principles. Each type serves unique applications across various fields.</p></p>
