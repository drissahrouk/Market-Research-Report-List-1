<p><strong>The Flooring Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Waterborne</li><li>Solventborne</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The flooring paint market comprises two primary types: waterborne and solventborne paints. Waterborne paints utilize water as the primary solvent, offering advantages such as low volatile organic compounds (VOCs), quick drying times, and easy cleanup. They are ideal for residential and light commercial applications. In contrast, solventborne paints use organic solvents, providing enhanced durability and resistance to harsh conditions, making them suitable for industrial and heavy-use environments. Each type caters to different performance requirements and user preferences.</p></p>
