<p><p>The Electrical Arc Furnace (EAF) power transformers market is categorized into two main types: direct EAF and indirect EAF. Direct EAF transformers supply power directly to the arc furnace, enabling efficient melting of scrap metal with minimal energy loss and swift response to load changes. Indirect EAF transformers, on the other hand, provide power through an intermediate system, allowing for better voltage regulation and flexibility in operations. Both types are crucial for optimizing steel production and improving operational efficiency in metallurgical processes.</p></p>
