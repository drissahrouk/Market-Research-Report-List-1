<p><p>The trailer portable toilets market comprises two primary types: single structure and composite structure portable toilets. Single structure portable toilets are made from a single molded unit, ensuring durability and ease of transport. They are commonly used for events and construction sites due to their straightforward design. In contrast, composite structure portable toilets consist of multiple materials, combining fiberglass, metal, and other elements for enhanced aesthetics and comfort. These are often preferred for long-term installations and special events, offering a more upscale experience.</p></p>
