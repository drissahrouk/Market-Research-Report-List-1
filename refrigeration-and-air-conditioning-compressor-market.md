<p><strong>The Refrigeration and Air Conditioning Compressor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Rotary-Screw Compressors</li><li>Reciprocating Compressors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The refrigeration and air conditioning compressor market features two primary types: rotary-screw compressors and reciprocating compressors. Rotary-screw compressors utilize two interlocking screws to compress refrigerant, offering smooth operation and high efficiency for large commercial applications. In contrast, reciprocating compressors use pistons to compress refrigerant in a cyclical motion, making them suitable for smaller systems and household use. Both types play crucial roles in cooling systems, with distinct advantages in efficiency, capacity, and application suitability.</p></p>
