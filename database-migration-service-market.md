<p><strong>The Database Migration Service Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Live Migration</li><li>Cold Migration</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Database Migration Service encompasses two primary market types: live migration and cold migration. Live migration allows for the transfer of databases while they remain operational, minimizing downtime and ensuring continuous access for users. In contrast, cold migration involves transferring databases that are offline, typically during scheduled maintenance windows, ensuring a complete data transfer without user activity. Each type serves different business needs, with live migration favoring environments requiring high availability and cold migration suited for less time-sensitive operations.</p></p>
