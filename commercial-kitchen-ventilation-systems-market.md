<p><p>Commercial kitchen ventilation systems ensure safety and air quality by removing heat, smoke, and odors. </p><p>Wall-mounted canopy hoods are attached to walls, suitable for linear cooking equipment. Island canopy hoods hang from the ceiling, ideal for open kitchen designs, providing 360-degree coverage. Proximity hoods are compact and fit closely to cooking appliances, perfect for limited spaces. Eyebrow hoods are low-profile and designed to work above specific cooking equipment. Other types may include under-cabinet hoods or custom solutions catering to unique kitchen layouts.</p></p>
