<p><ul><li>Passive Closure Devices</li><li>Active Closure Devices</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Arterial Incision Closure Device market is divided into two main types: passive and active closure devices. Passive closure devices rely on natural biological processes to achieve hemostasis, using materials that promote clotting without external intervention. Conversely, active closure devices employ mechanical systems or technology to apply pressure or seal the incision actively. Both types are designed to reduce complications and improve recovery times after procedures involving arterial access, offering varied options based on patient needs and procedural requirements.</p></p>
