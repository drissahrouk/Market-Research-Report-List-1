PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) hoses are renowned for their excellent chemical resistance and high-temperature stability, making them ideal for harsh environments. PFA (perfluoroalkoxy) hoses offer similar properties but with enhanced flexibility and transparency. Other materials may include various hybrid or engineered fluoropolymers, catering to specific industrial applications.</p></p>
