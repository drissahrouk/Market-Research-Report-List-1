<p><ul><li>Liquid Cooling and Heating</li><li>Air Cooling and Heating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Battery Thermal Management System (BTMS) market includes two primary types: liquid cooling and heating, and air cooling and heating. Liquid systems utilize fluids to efficiently transfer heat away from or towards the battery, ensuring optimal temperature control for performance and longevity. In contrast, air systems use air circulation to manage battery temperature, often being simpler and lighter but less efficient than liquid systems. Both types are essential for enhancing battery safety and efficiency in applications like electric vehicles and energy storage.</p></p>
