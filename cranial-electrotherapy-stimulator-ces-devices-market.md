<p><p>Cranial Electrotherapy Stimulator (CES) devices are designed for treating anxiety, depression, and sleep disorders through low electrical currents applied to the cranial region. The P-Stim device utilizes transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation (TENS) for pain relief and mood enhancement by stimulating specific auricular points. In contrast, E-pulse devices offer a neuromodulation approach, addressing various psychological and neurological conditions by delivering targeted electrical pulses. Both types aim to improve mental well-being with minimal invasiveness, appealing to a growing market in alternative therapies.</p></p>
