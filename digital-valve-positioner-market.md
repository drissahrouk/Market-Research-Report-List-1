<p><strong>The Digital Valve Positioner Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single Acting Positioner</li><li>Double Acting Positioner</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The digital valve positioner market is categorized into two main types: single acting and double acting positioners. Single acting positioners utilize a spring mechanism to return the valve to a default position, allowing for simpler control in applications where fail-safe operation is desired. In contrast, double acting positioners use pressure on both sides to provide more precise control, enabling faster and more accurate valve positioning. Both types are essential for optimizing process efficiency and reliability in various industrial applications.</p></p>
