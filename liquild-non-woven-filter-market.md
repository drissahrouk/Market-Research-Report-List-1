<p><strong>The Liquild Non-woven Filter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Meltblown</li><li>Needle Felt</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The liquid non-woven filter market comprises primarily two types: meltblown and needle felt. Meltblown filters are created by melting polymer fibers, resulting in a fine web structure ideal for capturing small particles, making them highly effective for filtration applications. Needle felt filters, on the other hand, are produced by bonding fibers using needles, which form a dense mat that offers enhanced durability and higher dirt-holding capacity, suitable for varying liquid filtration needs across industries. Both types cater to diverse filtration requirements.</p></p>
