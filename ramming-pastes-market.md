<p><p>The ramming paste market is primarily categorized into two types: warm type and cold type. Warm type ramming paste, typically applied at elevated temperatures, enhances bonding and performance in high-temperature applications like aluminum and copper smelting. In contrast, cold type ramming paste is used at ambient temperatures, offering convenience and ease of application for diverse refractory lining processes. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, aligning with specific operational conditions and performance requirements in metal production and refining industries.</p></p>
