<p><strong>The AR-HMD Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Based on Waveguides</li><li>Based on Curved Mirrors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The AR-HMD market can be classified into two main types based on technology: waveguides and curved mirrors. Waveguide-based AR-HMDs utilize thin optical devices to project images directly into the user's eyes, offering a lightweight and compact design suitable for a wide range of applications. In contrast, curved mirror-based AR-HMDs employ curved reflective surfaces to direct light and images, providing a different optical experience. Both types cater to various industries, enhancing user interactions in fields like gaming, training, and healthcare.</p></p>
