<p><p>The surgical sealants market is categorized into three primary types: natural/biological sealants, synthetic sealants, and semi-synthetic sealants. Natural sealants derive from biological sources, such as collagen or fibrin, offering biocompatibility and promoting tissue healing. Synthetic sealants are man-made, often composed of polymers like cyanoacrylate, providing strong adhesion and durability. Semi-synthetic sealants combine natural components with synthetic materials, leveraging the benefits of both to enhance performance and safety in surgical procedures, addressing various tissue types and healing requirements.</p></p>
