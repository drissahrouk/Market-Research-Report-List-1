<p><p>The Food Pulsed Electric Field (PEF) System market includes several key components: </p><p>1. **Pulse Generator**: Produces high-voltage pulses essential for food processing.</p><p>2. **Control Switch**: Regulates the pulse frequency and duration for optimal treatment.</p><p>3. **Feed Pump**: Moves food through the PEF system, ensuring consistent flow.</p><p>4. **Processing System**: Comprises the electrodes and chamber where the electric fields treat the food.</p><p>5. **Cooling System**: Maintains temperature control during processing to preserve food quality. </p><p>Together, these components enhance food safety, shelf life, and quality.</p></p>
