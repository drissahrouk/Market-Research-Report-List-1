<p><p>The 5G Fronthaul Wave Molecular System market includes three key types: CWDM (Coarse Wavelength Division Multiplexing), LWDM (Dense Wavelength Division Multiplexing), and MWDM (Medium Wavelength Division Multiplexing). CWDM uses wider channel spacing and is cost-effective for shorter distances. LWDM offers higher channel density for increased capacity, suited for urban networks. MWDM strikes a balance between the two, optimizing capacity and distance. Each type supports the high-speed, low-latency requirements of 5G infrastructure.</p></p>
