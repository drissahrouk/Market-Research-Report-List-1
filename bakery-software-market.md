<p><p>The bakery software market is categorized into two main types: cloud-based and on-premises solutions. Cloud-based software is hosted online, allowing bakeries to access their systems from anywhere, promoting flexibility and ease of use while reducing IT infrastructure costs. Conversely, on-premises solutions involve software installed directly on the bakery's local servers, providing greater control over data and compliance but requiring more maintenance and upfront investment. Both types cater to varying business needs and operational preferences within the bakery industry.</p></p>
