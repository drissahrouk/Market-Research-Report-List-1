<p><ul><li>Contact Visual IR Thermometer</li><li>Non-contact Visual IR Thermometer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Visual IR Thermometers market is categorized into two types: contact and non-contact thermometers. Contact visual IR thermometers require physical contact with the surface being measured, providing accurate temperature readings through direct thermal conduction. In contrast, non-contact visual IR thermometers measure temperature from a distance by detecting infrared radiation emitted from an object. This type offers quick results and is ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Both types serve crucial roles in industrial, medical, and scientific applications.</p></p>
