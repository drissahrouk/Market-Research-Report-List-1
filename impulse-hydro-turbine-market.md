<p><ul><li>Pelton Hydro Turbine</li><li>Cross Flow Hydro Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The impulse hydro turbine market comprises various turbine types, primarily focusing on Pelton and Cross Flow designs. Pelton turbines utilize a series of buckets on a wheel, converting the kinetic energy of high-velocity water jets into mechanical energy, ideal for high head applications. In contrast, Cross Flow turbines feature a cylindrical design where water flows perpendicularly across blades, effectively harnessing energy from lower head sites. Both turbine types are crucial for optimizing energy production in diverse hydropower projects.</p></p>
