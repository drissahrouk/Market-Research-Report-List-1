<p><p>The Food Allergy and Intolerance Products Market can be divided into two primary types: Immunoglobulin E (IgE) Mediated Food Allergy and Non-IgE Mediated Food Allergy. IgE mediated allergies involve an immediate immune response triggered by allergens, leading to symptoms like hives or anaphylaxis. Non-IgE mediated allergies, on the other hand, typically result in delayed reactions, affecting the gastrointestinal system and causing symptoms such as abdominal pain or diarrhea. Understanding these distinctions is crucial for developing targeted solutions and products for affected individuals.</p></p>
