<p><p>The deep hyperthermia devices market is primarily categorized into two types: microwave hyperthermia devices and ultrasound hyperthermia devices. Microwave hyperthermia devices utilize microwave energy to heat targeted tissues, enhancing the effectiveness of cancer treatments by increasing blood flow and promoting tumor cell destruction. Conversely, ultrasound hyperthermia devices employ high-frequency sound waves to achieve similar therapeutic effects, allowing for precise targeting and minimal damage to surrounding healthy tissues. Both modalities aim to improve patient outcomes in oncology and other therapeutic areas by elevating tissue temperatures.</p></p>
